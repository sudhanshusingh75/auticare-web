Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 04, 2025

Auticare ["we", "our", or "us"] values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use the Auticare mobile application ["App"].

By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Data We Collect

1.1. Personal Data

We collect the following personally identifiable information:

  • Full Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
  • Email Address
  • Username
  • Hashed Password
  • Optional: Profile Picture, Bio, Address

1.2. Assessment and Usage Data

We collect non-personal usage information to understand how users interact with the App. This includes:

  • Child’s behavioral responses
  • Indian Scale for Assessment of Autism [ISAA] scores
  • Progress tracking data
  • Uploaded images and content [e.g., community posts]

Note: The App does not access GPS or real-time device location. You may optionally input your address manually in your profile.

2. Information Related to Children

Auticare is designed for adults [18+], but assessments may be conducted on behalf of children aged 2 and above.

  • We do not allow minors to register or create their own accounts.
  • Users must confirm they are legally authorized to provide information on behalf of a child.
  • Data collected about children through assessments is stored securely and used solely for intended educational and therapeutic purposes.

We do not knowingly collect information directly from children. If we become aware that data was provided without proper consent, we will delete it promptly.

3. How We Use Data

Your information may be used for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To conduct and store autism assessments [e.g., ISAA]
  • To track child development and assessment history
  • To provide educational resources and learning materials
  • To enable community features [e.g., sharing posts or images]
  • To improve the App’s functionality, usability, and user experience
  • To ensure compliance with our legal obligations and Terms of Use

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. In the future, we may introduce optional features that involve collaboration with therapists or healthcare professionals. You will be explicitly asked to opt-in and provide consent before any data is shared for these purposes.

We may disclose data only to:

4.1. Affiliates and Subsidiaries

We may share data with affiliated companies and subsidiaries for internal business operations and support.

4.2. Service Providers

We engage third parties such as:

  • Payment processors
  • Data analytics services
  • Notification and communications platforms

These providers are granted access only as needed to perform services on our behalf.

4.3. Legal and Regulatory Disclosures

We may disclose data if required by law, legal process, or to comply with law enforcement or government requests.

4.4. Business Transfers

If Auticare is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, we may transfer your data to the relevant third party as part of the transaction.

5. Data Storage and Retention

5.1

All user data is stored in Google Firestore, a secure cloud-based database.

5.2

Uploaded media [images, learning videos] is stored in Supabase Storage.

5.3

We utilize the built-in security features of these platforms [e.g., encrypted at rest and in transit].

5.4

At this time, we do not apply additional proprietary encryption, but we may implement such measures in future updates.

5.5. Retention Duration

We retain both personal and usage data:

  • For as long as necessary to provide services
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To resolve disputes and enforce agreements

5.6. Deletion Policy

When data is no longer required, we delete or anonymize it using commercially reasonable methods.

Your Responsibility: You are solely responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials. We recommend using strong passwords and changing them periodically.

6. Your Choices and Control

6.1. Data Access and Requests

You may request:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent to data processing
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Object to certain types of data use [e.g., marketing]

6.2. Limitations

We may refuse or limit your request if:

  • Retention is required by law
  • It compromises the security or functionality of the App
  • It affects other users’ rights or disrupts ongoing investigations

6.3. Request Process

To make a privacy-related request, contact us at support@auticare.com. We may require verification before fulfilling your request.

7. Security Measures

7.1. Technical Safeguards

We implement commercially reasonable security controls including:

  • Data encryption in transit
  • Access controls and user authentication
  • Routine security audits

7.2. Disclaimer

No method of transmission or storage is fully secure. We do not guarantee absolute security and disclaim liability for unauthorized access beyond our control.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

8.1. Use of Technologies

We use the following technologies:

  • Cookies for session management
  • SDKs for analytics and crash reporting
  • Device and OS-level identifiers

8.2. Purpose of Tracking

Tracking helps us:

  • Maintain core App functionality
  • Improve performance and usability
  • Serve relevant in-app messages and promotions

8.3. Opt-Out Limitations

Essential tracking [e.g., for login sessions or order history] cannot be disabled. You may adjust device-level privacy settings to limit non-essential data collection.

9. Third-Party Services

The App may link to or integrate with third-party platforms such as educational video providers or community tools. This Privacy Policy does not cover data practices of third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before interacting with their services.

10. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly allow users under 18 to create accounts. If we discover that a minor has registered an account, we will take steps to delete it.

11. Policy Changes

11.1. Right to Modify

We may modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes take effect upon posting the revised version within the App or on our website.

11.2. User Acknowledgement

Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
